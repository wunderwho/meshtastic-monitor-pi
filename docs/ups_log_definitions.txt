UPS Log Field Definitions

ts
  Unix epoch timestamp when the sample was recorded.

voltage_v
  Output voltage measured on the UPS rail.

current_a
  Current measured on the UPS output rail.
  Positive = charging from mains.
  Negative = battery discharge.

power_w_measured
  Raw power measured by the INA219 on the UPS output rail.

load_w
  Estimated real system power consumption.
  Calculated by multiplying measured output power by a correction factor
  to approximate battery-side power draw.

avg_power_w
  Rolling average of estimated load while on battery.

remaining_wh
  Estimated remaining battery capacity in watt-hours.

runtime_hours
  Estimated remaining runtime based on remaining energy and average load.

on_battery
  Boolean indicating whether the node is running on battery power.

POWER_LOST
  Logged when the system detects a transition from mains to battery.

POWER_RESTORED
  Logged when mains power returns.
  Includes outage duration in seconds.
